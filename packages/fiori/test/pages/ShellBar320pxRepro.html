ShellBar at 320px width — Application Title chevron repro

An FLP-style application with a 320px screen width may be unable to display the Application Title button. Even the arrow-only (chevron) version of the title button can disappear due to limited horizontal space when two start buttons, the SAP logo, the overflow button, and the profile avatar are all rendered.
